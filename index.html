Who are Collect?


Collect wants to encourage people to recycle more. To achieve this, we want to establish our new recycling stations around the world. By supporting Collect you will also support United Nations Sustainable Development Goals:



GOAL 13



Collect Stations

By using our Collect Stations, you will contribute to less consumption and emissions. The station has two different containers. One container for glass and metal packaging, and one container for plastic bottles and cans.
