http://student.cs.hioa.no/~s341894/FinalProject/app/information-sources.html To recycle is to be environmentally conscious. By recycling, you ensure high-quality recycling of cans and plastic bottles. What does this mean? This means that the bottles and cans can be remade to new bottles and cans, or new plastic packaging. By doing this, we will reduce the use of raw materials, we reduce emissions, and we use significantly less energy in the production of new products.
